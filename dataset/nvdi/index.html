Normalized Vegetation Index

  1. datasets
  2. normalized vegetation index

this resource comes from a zip archive with a several shape files that show the normalized vegetation index (NDVI) in the different areas (sidewalks, islets, parcels, planning and subvials).
Original source
here
The data is collected in Spring 2018.

  • ndvi_sidewalks_islets.zip (47Mb)
    description: normalized vegetation index (NDVI) for the sidewalks, and islets of Madrid
  • ndvi_parcels.zip 42Mb)
    description: normalized vegetation index (NDVI) for the parcels of Madrid
  • ndvi_subviales.zip (35Mb)
    description: normalized vegetation index (NDVI) for the subvials of Madrid
    https://geoportal.madrid.es/IDEAM_WBGEOPORTAL/dataset.iam?id=c02e181e-6d78-11e9-a351-40a8f0462086
All the files are in geojson format projected in WGS84 - epsg:4326
